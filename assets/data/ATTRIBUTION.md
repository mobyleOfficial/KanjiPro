# Data Attribution

The kanji data bundled in `kanji.json` is derived from **KANJIDIC2** (kanji readings,
meanings, stroke counts, JLPT levels) and **JMdict** (the usage example words: word,
reading, meaning), both provided via the [kanjiapi.dev](https://kanjiapi.dev) API.

KANJIDIC2 and JMdict are the property of the
[Electronic Dictionary Research and Development Group (EDRDG)](https://www.edrdg.org/)
and are used in accordance with the Group's
[licence](https://www.edrdg.org/edrdg/licence.html).
