# Data Attribution

The kanji data bundled in `kanji.json` is derived from **KANJIDIC2**, provided via the
[kanjiapi.dev](https://kanjiapi.dev) API.

KANJIDIC2 is the property of the
[Electronic Dictionary Research and Development Group (EDRDG)](https://www.edrdg.org/)
